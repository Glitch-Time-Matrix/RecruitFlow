# Product

<!-- impeccable:product-schema 1 -->

## Platform

web

## Users
Elite Executive and Technical Talent looking for confidential placement, and world-class corporate employers seeking high-caliber professionals.

## Product Purpose
Connecting exceptional talent with world-class enterprises through a confidential, high-trust digital agency platform.

## Positioning
A premium, highly-vetted staffing platform that offers executive headhunting and specialized technical placement with unmatched speed and absolute confidentiality.

## Operating Context
- Web-based desktop & mobile interface.
- Candidate portal for submitting resumes and tracking profiles.
- Employer portal for submitting hiring requests and viewing talent pipelines.
- Searchable active job directory.

## Capabilities and Constraints
- Single-page React application with client-side routing.
- Styled using Tailwind CSS with premium Cosmos.so aesthetics.
- Client-side mock data with no external backend integration (Vercel deployable).

## Brand Commitments
- Name: RecruitFlow (Staffing Agency).
- Voice: Elite, professional, high-trust, and authoritative.
- Design: Cosmos.so-inspired minimalist vibe, clean typography, executive tone, and airy spacing.

## Evidence on Hand
- Curated list of mock jobs in `src/data.ts`.
- Interactive components: Sparkles hero, infinite scroll testimonials, stacked features card layout.

## Product Principles
1. **Confidentiality First**: Protect candidate and employer data transparency.
2. **Authoritative Simplicity**: Maintain a clean, minimalist executive aesthetic.
3. **Responsive Speed**: Ensure zero-jank transitions and mobile-first touch reliability.
